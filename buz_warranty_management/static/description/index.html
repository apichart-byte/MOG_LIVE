Warranty Management System

Complete warranty management for Odoo 17

Key Features

Product-Level Configuration

Configure warranty terms directly on products with flexible duration and conditions

Automatic Card Generation

Warranty cards are automatically created when products are delivered to customers

Claim Management

Handle both under-warranty and out-of-warranty claims with integrated workflows

Professional Reporting

Generate professional warranty certificates and claim forms with a single click. All documents include customer information, product details, and warranty terms.

Smart Integration

Seamlessly integrates with Sales, Stock, and Accounting modules. Track warranty claims, generate out-of-warranty quotations, and maintain complete audit trails.
